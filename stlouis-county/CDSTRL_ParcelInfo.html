Section 1 Overview
Originator St Louis County Auditors office and the City of Duluth
Title CDSTRL_ParcelInfo
Abstract For ArcCatalog users U:\GIS-Users\Metadata\Parcelinfo_Tax_Parcel_Test.html Parcels are edited in the parcel Fabric and exported to ParcelInfo daily.All fields are generated from MCIS except 'ACREAGE' and 'Ownership'. 'ACREAGE' field is GIS calculated acres and NOT deeded acres. If discrepancies are found, please contact Auditor Department.
Purpose Parcel boundaries for Current Tax Year. A SQL view is created by merging the spatial parcels (PIN #) from Fabric.sde to the MCIS (PIN #). This Feature is what drives all the Land Records Analysis for the County.
